Join the Microsoft MakeCode Insiders Program

Share your opinion. Shape an experience.

To be eligible for the program, students must meet the following criteria.

  • Aged 13-17 years old (born between December 2004 and July 2008)
  • Live in the United States
  • Read and speak English fluently
  • Be available for an hour-long virtual session every week for three months
  • Be available to dedicate up to two hours of additional time each week to Insider projects
  • No coding experience required

  1. First, read through the Insiders Program Booklet for a description of what it means to be a MakeCode Insider.

  2. Next, fill out the Insider Application and submit it to us by August 6th, 2021. (Don't forget to include your video or essay!)

  3. Finalists will be scheduled for interviews the week of 8/8/21 - 8/15/21.

    4. Apply Now!
